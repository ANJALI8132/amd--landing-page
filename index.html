ABOUT

ALHIND ACADEMY

Join alhind academy and be transformed as a complete global professonal

Alhind Academy, a subsidiary of Alhind Group is a prominent Travel & Tourism and Aviation Academy in Kerala - India. Alhind Academy is an IATA Authorized Training Centre (ATC) for International Travel & Tourism, Aviation, Cargo and GDS courses. Academy has a track record of 100% pass rate and placement.

IATA recognizes Alhind Academy as Top Performing Authorized Training Centers (ATC) and Premier Circle Worldwide Top 10. Alhind Academy achieved impressive results in training a large number of students, demonstrated loyalty to IATA training programs and commitment to student success reflected by high student examination passing rates.

Achieving high educational excellence, high moral values and discipline is the prime motto of the Academy. Academy is a venture to fulfil the dreams of candidates aspiring for a career in the Travel & Tourism Industry. As one of the best aviation colleges in Kerala, we offer a variety of courses to choose from.
